Professional Comedy Clubs

Logo for Helium Comedy Club

Comedy has been around for years, and has gone through many phases of evolution. However, some say you can't beat the original style.
There are many opportunities to see a good comedy show in a classic, club setting. You could visit:

  • Helium Comedy Club in Brentwood
  • Funny Bone in Westport
  • The Laugh Lounge in Florissant
  • The Improv Shop in the Grove

Venue Shows

The Ready Room, located in St Louis's Grove district

Maybe the club scene isn't your thing. You'd like to be able to say you saw Kyle Kinane and Lana Del Rey on the same stage (not at the same time, that would be weird).
In that case, you should check out an independently booked show. This could happen anywhere:

  • The Pageant
  • The Ready Room
  • Blueberry Hill
The possibilities are endless!

Independent Showcases

"But Sam!", you say. "I've seen plenty of shows at concert halls and comedy clubs. I just want to go hang out at a comfortable bar somewhere and chill. Too bad they can't do comedy at a bar." Oh sweet, sweet reader. You can see awesome independent shows at bars in St. Louis every night of the week!:

  • Impolite Company at The Crack Fox
  • Yours, Mine, & Ours at Gezellig
  • Coffee Break at Foam
