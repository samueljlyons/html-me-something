When many people think about cities known for comedy, their thoughts migrate to New York City, LA, and Chicago.But what if I told you that there is grade a comedy to be seen within a 10-mile radius of the Arch? St. Louis has long been a destination for those interested in great comedy. From clubs rich with history to independent bar shows, and everything in between, you can find a good laugh in the Lou. "That sounds preposterous", you might say? Well, get ready to be amazed.

Professional Comedy Clubs

Comedy has been around for years, and has gone through many phases of evolution. However, some say you can't beat the original style. Show up with your ticket, and be escorted to your seat by an usher. You're so close to the stage, you could reach out and touch the performer. Please don't though, without their explicit consent.
There are many opportunities to see a good comedy show in a classic, club setting. You could visit:

  • Helium Comedy Club in Brentwood
  • Funny Bone in Westport
  • The Laugh Lounge in Florissant
  • The Improv Shop in the Grove

Independent Showcases

"But Sam!", you say. "I've seen plenty of shows at concert halls and comedy clubs. I just want to go hang out at a comfortable bar somewhere and chill. Too bad they can't do comedy at a bar." Oh sweet, sweet reader. You can see awesome independent shows at bars in St. Louis every night of the week!:

  • Impolite Company at The Crack Fox
  • Yours, Mine, & Ours at Gezellig
  • Coffee Break at Foam
